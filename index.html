Hello, I'm Celeste Van Tonder

an aspiring developer and designer.

I’m currently studying to become a full stack developer at the Open Window Institute of Arts and Digital Sciences. This portfolio showcases some of my latest projects in Development and Interaction Design. I’m situated in Pretoria.

Horizon Cruisers

Horizon Cruisers is a cruise booking website. There is a wide variety of trips to choose from. Users can filter and sort the trips to find the best match. Then, they can add the trip to their cart or remove it if they want. It also has a weather widget that pulls weather data straight from Openweather API. This terms focus was on jQuery and API functionality.

SuperSubs Pizza

SuperSubs Pizza allows users to build their own pizza. They can choose from a wide variety of toppings, bases, sauces, and they can even give their pizza a name. Since pizza is the healthiest food on the planet there is even a calory checker. This term was an introduction to Javascript. We also focused on local and session storage.

Github link

Strava Mockup Website

These are the UI designs and prototype for a mockup site of Strava. We used the brand's guidelines to prepare us for the industry. This term was all about feedback. We specifically focused on the sign up, login, and contact us pages. If you scan the QR code it will take you to real Strava site.

SPARQ ENERGY

SPARQ Energy is an e-commerce website for gaming (energy) drinks. The users can sort and filter between products as well as add or remove items from their cart. This term's brief was to create an e-commerce site for a specific audience and industry. I also included more interactivity and dynamic content through animation.
